Hello and welcome to my homepage! I am currently an Ambizione Fellow at LASTRO.


From 2021 to 2022 I was a CITA National Fellow at Western and from 2017 to 2021 I was a postdoctoral fellow at the Research School of Astronomy and Astrophysics at Mount Stromlo Observatory. I received my PhD in 2017 from the University of Crete (UoC) under the supervision of Kostas Tassis.


In 2020 I was awarded the European Astronomical Society's MERAC Prize for the best PhD thesis in theoretical Astrophysics.


When I am not working I like to spend my days on the beautiful island of Ithaki in Greece, where I am originally from.
